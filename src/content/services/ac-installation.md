Most underperforming AC systems weren't broken — they were installed wrong. Oversized units short-cycle and fail to remove humidity. Undersized units run constantly and burn through electricity. Bad ductwork wastes up to 30% of the cooling you're paying for.

At Dalelis, we start every installation with a Manual J load calculation, inspect your existing ductwork, and commission the system properly — so you get the comfort and efficiency the equipment is actually capable of delivering.
